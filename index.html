At Illumina, we believe in the transformative power of light and fragrance. Each illumina candle is meticulously handcrafted using premium natural soy wax and carefully selected fragance oils.

Our commitment to quality ensures that every candle not only emanates a captivating scent but also burns evenly, providing hours of enchanting illumination. From the initial concept to the final product, our candles are a reflection of our dedication to craftsmanship.

We understand that fragrance is a personal experience. That's why our collection features an array of distinctive scents, from comforting notes of lavendar to the invigorating aromas of citrus. Whether you're unwinding after a long day or creating an inviting atmosphere for guests, Illumina has the perfect candle to complement every moment.

Made from 100% vegan, cruelty-free soy wax, our candles ensure a cleaner burn than traditional paraffin wax candles, reducing our carbon footprint. Infused with natural essential oils, our candles provide a fragrant and eco-friendly alternative to synthetic oils: we are committed to using only plant-based scents. Our sustainable commitment also extends to our packaging, crafted from recycled materials and fully recyclable, minimising landfill impact.
