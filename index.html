At Illumina, we believe in the transformative power of light and fragrance. Each illumina candle is meticulously handcrafted using premium natural soy wax and carefully selected fragance oils.

Our commitment to quality ensures that every candle not only emanates a captivating scent but also burns evenly, providing hours of enchanting illumination. From the initial concept to the final product, our candles are a reflection of our dedication to craftsmanship.

We understand that fragrance is a personal experience. That's why our collection features an array of distinctive scents, from comforting notes of lavendar to the invigorating aromas of citrus. Whether you're unwinding after a long day or creating an inviting atmosphere for guests, Illumina has the perfect candle to complement every moment.

Our Story

Our story began with a shared passion for creating moments of warmth and tranquility, and it evolved into the creation of candles that captivate the senses and elevate the ambiance of any space.

Founded by a group of friends, who met through Young Enterprise, Illumina is the culmination of creativity, friendship, and a deep appreciation for the art of candle making. Inspired by the idea that a flickering flame and a delightful aroma have the ability to turn ordinary moments into extroadinary experiences, we embarked on a journey to craft candles that embody sophistication, style, and serenity.


As stewards of both creativity and nature, we are committed to sustainability. Our candles are crafted with eco-friendly practices, using responsibly sourced materials. We believe in leaving a positive impact on the environment, so you can enjoy our candles with the peace of mind that comes from supporting a brand that cares.
