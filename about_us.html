Our Story

Our story began with a shared passion for creating moments of warmth and tranquility, and it evolved into the creation of candles that captivate the senses and elevate the ambiance of any space.

Founded by a group of friends, who met through Young Enterprise, Illumina is the culmination of creativity, friendship, and a deep appreciation for the art of candle making. Inspired by the idea that a flickering flame and a delightful aroma have the ability to turn ordinary moments into extroadinary experiences, we embarked on a journey to craft candles that embody sophistication, style, and serenity.
